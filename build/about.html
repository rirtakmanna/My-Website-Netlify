Let's design the digital world in new way

Hey!! I'm Rirtak manna and it's my mission is to design the digital world in a new way, in a better way. I love creating websites that resonate and connect with the target audiences. I spend my entire day, practically every day, experimenting with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I always tinker on “creative” things, from clicking and editing abstract photos for my Instagram page to designing a website for a client.

Well, I am a commerce student cleverly disguised as a web developer. My knowledge for business helps me to understand the market, which ultimately helps me to strategize for website designing, which eventually helps my Client to reach the target audience.

From HTML, CSS, JavaScript, sass, bootstrap 4, and JQuery, I can even handle unknown technologies like Webflow. I work even in software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and even new programs like Adobe Dimensions, XD and Figma. I constantly work on developing new skills with the latest software and technologies to help clients achieve a modern, sophisticated look in digital realms.

I’m curious and I enjoy work which challenges me to learn something new and stretch me in a different direction and reach my maximum potential.
